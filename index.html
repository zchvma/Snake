About Snake Game

Welcome to the Advanced Snake Game! This is a modern take on the classic Snake game with additional features and challenges.

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys or on-screen controls to navigate the snake
  • Eat food to grow longer and earn points
  • Avoid colliding with walls and your own body
  • The game gets progressively harder as you level up

Special Features

  • Portals: Teleport your snake to different parts of the map
  • Bonuses: Collect special items for various effects:
    • Speed Up - Temporarily increases snake speed
    • Speed Down - Temporarily decreases snake speed
    • Destroyer - Destroys nearby walls
    • Inversion - Inverts your controls
  • Poison Zones: Avoid having your entire snake in these areas
  • Expanding Map: The game world is larger than what's visible on screen
  • Minimap: Helps you navigate the larger game world
  • Skins: Change your snake's appearance

Game concept based on the classic Snake game, with modern enhancements inspired by various implementations across gaming history.
